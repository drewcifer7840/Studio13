About Studio 13

Studio 13 is Lake Norman’s premier music workshop located in the heart of Old Town Cornelius, NC at the historic Oak Street Mill. We provide a fully-equipped rehearsal studio for musicians and bands including a PA system, house drum kit, keyboard, concession area and lounge.

We have created an extremely positive and relaxed environment all geared towards developing your skills, whether you are an absolute beginner trying to get off the ground, or an accomplished group looking to move to the next level. Book your next band rehearsal or private show at Studio 13 and see what you’ve been missing! Rates starting at $25/hr. Contact us for inquiries or scheduling.
